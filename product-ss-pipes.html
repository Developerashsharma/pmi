Stainless Steel Pipes & Tubes

Seamless & Welded (ERW, EFW)

Ready Stock MTC Enclosed Hydro Tested

Paramount Metal Industries offers a comprehensive range of SS Pipes and Tubes designed for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Available in Seamless, Welded, and ERW forms, our pipes are ideal for critical industrial applications in Oil & Gas, Pharmaceuticals, and Heat Exchangers.

  • Key Grades: 304/304L, 316/316L, 321H, 904L.
  • Testing: 100% Hydro-Tested, Eddy Current, PMI.
  • Finish: Matt, Mirror, Bead Removed, Electropolished.

High-Performance Piping Solutions

We supply SS Pipes in both Seamless (for high-pressure environments) and Welded (for low to moderate pressure) configurations. Our inventory supports a wide array of schedules (SCH 5 to SCH XXS) to meet diverse mechanical and structural requirements. We also provide specialized Electro-Polished (EP) tubes for the pharmaceutical and dairy industries.

All our materials are sourced from reputable manufacturers and come with complete traceability and NABL approved lab reports.

Standard ASTM A312, ASTM A213, ASTM A269, ASTM A249, ASTM A554
Grades TP 202, 304, 304L, 304H, 316, 316L, 316Ti, 317L, 321, 347, 904L
Type Seamless, ERW, EFW, Welded, Rectangular, Square, Oval
Size 1/8" NB to 48" NB (Nominal Bore)
Schedule SCH 5, SCH 10, SCH 20, SCH 40, SCH 80, SCH 160, SCH XXS
Length Single Random, Double Random, Cut Length (Up to 12 Meters)
Form Round, Square, Rectangular, Hydraulic, Horn Tubes, Coil, U-Tubes
