Industrial Flanges

SORF, WNRF, BLRF, Threaded, Lap Joint

Ready Stock Class 150# to 2500# IBR Certified

We are a leading manufacturer of high-pressure Flanges, ensuring leak-proof connections in critical pipeline systems. From standard ANSI/ASME configurations to custom large-diameter flanges, we deliver precision and reliability.

  • Materials: Stainless Steel, Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel.

Connecting Industries Seamessly

Our flange inventory encompasses Slip-On (SORF) for easy alignment, Weld Neck (WNRF) for high-stress applications, and Blind Flanges (BLRF) for sealing pipe ends. We create flanges that withstand extreme pressure and temperature variations.

We strictly adhere to dimensional standards like ASME B16.5 (up to 24") and ASME B16.47 (Series A & B for larger sizes).

Standard ANSI/ASME B16.5, B16.47 Series A & B, DIN, BS-10
