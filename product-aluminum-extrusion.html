Aluminum Extrusions & Pipes

Round Bars, Flat Bars, Channels, Angles, Pipes

Ready Stock 6061-T6 / 6063-T6 Anodized Available

Paramount Metal Industries offers high-strength Aluminum Extrusions and Profiles. Our range includes aerospace-grade alloys and architectural sections known for their lightweight nature, corrosion resistance, and exceptional strength-to-weight ratio.

  • Alloys: 6061, 6063, 6082, 5083, 7075.
  • Certifications: AS9100, MTC.
  • Service: Cut-to-size, Anodizing, Powder Coating.

Versatility Meets Strength

We stock a wide variety of Aluminum products. 6063-T6 is ideal for architectural applications like window frames and railings due to its excellent surface finish. 6082-T6 and 7075-T6 are structural alloys used in heavy stress applications like crane trusses and aerospace components.

Our Aluminum Pipes and Tubes are available in both Seamless (drawn) and Porthole (extruded) varieties.

Grades 1100, 2014, 2024, 5052, 5083, 5086, 6061, 6063, 6082, 7075
Standard ASTM B221, ASTM B209, ASTM B210, AMS 4000 series
Forms Round Bar, Flat Bar, Angle, Channel, Beam, Tee, Pipe, Tube, Plate, Sheet, Coil
Temper O, T3, T4, T6, T651, H111, H32
Finish Mill Finish, Anodized (Clear/Black/Gold), Powder Coated
  • Aerospace: Structural frames, fuselage components (7075/2024).
  • Marine: Boat hulls, decks (5083/5086).
  • Architecture: Facades, window sections, scaffolding.
  • Transport: Truck bodies, railway wagons.
