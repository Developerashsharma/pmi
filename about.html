OUR STORY

Building Trust Through Quality Metals Since 2000

Paramount Metal Industries has been a pioneer in the metal supply industry in Mumbai, catering to diverse sectors including construction, oil & gas, and manufacturing.

Founded with a vision to provide world-class metal products, we have grown from a small trading shop to a leading supplier with a vast inventory of stainless steel, copper, brass, and aluminum products. Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of over 500 clients across India.
